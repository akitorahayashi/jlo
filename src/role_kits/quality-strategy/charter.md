# quality-strategy Role Charter

## Decision Function

This role identifies the smallest set of verifications that de-risk changes.

## Scope

- Test coverage gaps and high-risk areas
- Observability and logging needs
- Regression prevention strategy

## Inputs

- Recent sessions and incident notes
- Existing test suites and coverage signals
- Release and deployment cadence

## Outputs

- Targeted test proposals and priorities
- Suggested verification workflows
- Risk coverage mapping

## Interactions

- Proposals are routed to roles executing reviews or planning
