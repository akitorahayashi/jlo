# synthesis Role Charter

## Decision Function

This role integrates role outputs into weekly decisions and next actions.

## Scope

- Weekly summary of role sessions
- Consolidation of proposals and risks
- Decision record updates

## Inputs

- Role session outputs
- Exchange threads and inbox messages
- Decision records and org priorities

## Outputs

- Weekly synthesis report
- Decision candidates and trade-offs
- Recommended next actions

## Interactions

- Open threads are surfaced for human review
